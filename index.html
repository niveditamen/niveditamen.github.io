About Me

I am a third year undergraduate pursuing a Bachelor's of Science in Wildlife Biology and Conservation at the University of Alaska Fairbanks. I am from the Bay Area in California and moved to Fairbanks in 2022.

I am currently working on the Inbre Pilot project “Mitochondrial Suppression in a Human-sized Hibernator” in Kristin O’Brien’s lab under Dr. Rice. This semester, we are working on determining the activity in mitochondrial complex I of spring, summer, and hibernating black bear tissue.
